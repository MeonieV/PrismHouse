Prism GuestHouse

Welcome to Prism House

Need a place to stay for the night? Well Prism house has all the rooms in each colour of the rainbow. We offer good quality and soft blankets with free internet and coffee. Come and sleep in a colourful room where your safety is important to us. The place is quiet and calm with friendly staff. If you bring children, there is a nice playground right within the gates.


For more information go to the About page or the Contact page.

What we offer

We offer various coloured rooms with different sizes and styles. Our goal is to keep our guests comfortable and safe with many entertaining options. Each room comes with air conditioning, a flat screen tv and private bathroom. There is a mini fridge in each room with a kettle and coffee. The kitchens are shared between the rooms and with room service.
